About Me

I am a Grade 12 coder and I like to code whenever I can. I mainly like algorithmic coding, but am interested in all aspects, and enjoy taking on new projects whenever possible.

Recently I have become interested in Machine Learning because of the YouTuber Code Bullet (if you have not seen him I suggest you check him out). I have had some experience with it before at a camp, but never got to see the underlying concepts of it in full detail.

Apart from Machine Learning and Algorithmic Coding I have done coding using many different libraries and in lots of different languages, including: Python, Java, C++, JS etc. Feel free to check out some of my repositories that are listed below (some of them are really bad as they were rushed, so please don't judge me too harshly!)
